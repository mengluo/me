Robot Orphan Workforce

Robot Orphan Workforce is a 3D building simulator game implemented in Unity3D. In the game, you own a construction company and you get those little cute orphan robots work for you. The orphans were abandoned by their home factory because their poor building skill. With your instructions and love, they will help you build different buildings out of blocks made of different material. Their building skills becomes better and better as they construct more buildings. And there is a small economy loop going on in the game because you need money to oil and maintain orphans and buy blocks and you can sell the buildings to get more money.

Escape the Island

This is a game level made by UDK and it was designed based on Alcatraz Island, in San Francisco Bay. The level is a prison full of horror, coldness, unfeelingness and mess, which is located in an isolated island. You start the game with hope in your heart of escaping the island. But the result is not always as what you are expecting...

Aerial Salvation

Aerial Salvation is a 2D flying adventure game developed by Unity3d for Android platform. This game is designed based on a piece of paint in Worcester Art Museum and it is trying to convey regret and grief in wars.

Abandoned Castle

This is a game level designed in UDK to present an eerie environment to players. Meng uses UDK cinematic tool to present a unique experience of walk-though of the game level.

Spark

Spark is an arcade style R/C kart racing game inspired by classics such as Mario Kart and Re-Volt. And this is a game prototype. You can play this game with your friends through LAN.

Whack It

Whack It is a Whac-A-Mole like game but the moles are replaced by the cubes and spheres. A board of 8 markers is used as a plane to render cubes in 6 positions randomly. Players can simply play the game with their hands with no need to use a keyboard, a mouse or any other controllers. It was programmed in C with ARToolkit based on OpenGL .

3D Firefighter Game

A third person game that takes place in a burning house. Moving fire is your enemy and fire extinguisher is your weapon. You must put out fire and save all victims in limited time.

3D Superman Adventure

The first game created by Meng in Unity3D as his undergrad graduation project. It is a three-level arcade type game inspired by classic space shooter game.

GO Fire

GO Fire is a game developed using augmented reality technology in Unity3D. It allows players to interact with the virtual game character by markers. It was programmed in C with Nyartoolkit.
